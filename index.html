Final Challenge

Write a mini AI product brief, then compare your thinking.

Your Challenge

Design an AI feature for a meal planning app: weekly plans based on dietary restrictions, budget, and available ingredients.

Framework Answer

AI Approach

An LLM with structured JSON output + RAG over a verified recipe database. Use the LLM for constraint reasoning (dietary, budget, ingredient matching) and the DB as ground truth. Balances flexibility with reliability.

Top Risk

Hallucinated recipes — incorrect ingredients or cooking times (safety risk). Mitigate: ground all recipes in the DB, add a validation layer, show "verified" badges, let users flag issues.

Two Metrics

Plan Acceptance Rate — % of plans accepted without major edits. Shopping List Completion Rate — % of ingredients users actually add to their cart. Both measure end-to-end value.

Failure Handling

Graceful degradation: (1) If constraints conflict, satisfy the most important and explicitly note what was relaxed. (2) Offer single-meal swaps instead of full regeneration. (3) Fall back to curated templates for common diets. Never show an error — always offer something useful.
